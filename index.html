About Caroline

A career built across
countries & industries.

Caroline studied Communications — which everyone thought was unconventional for someone from a geography background, but it launched an extraordinary career. She was raised in Scotland, Holland and Oman, and has worked across Spain and Kenya.

She trained as a copywriter at JWT advertising in Nairobi, learning to write for the full breadth of audiences. She went on to be part of the communications team behind the groundbreaking launch of BBC iPlayer, before working across multiple government departments as a security-cleared communications specialist.

Today she continues to be drawn to fascinating projects — bringing senior-level thinking with a freelancer's flexibility.
